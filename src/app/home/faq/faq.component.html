How can I Place Order?

There are two ways to Place Order

  By visiting our site https://powertextools.com/

 On the main page, Login your username and password

 Click on Dashboard

 Click on Orders you can select the products by using category & Sub Category & Modelno then Enter No.of Cartoons.

 if you add multiple products click Add button.

 once complete the order items selection then click on place order.

 Select Delivary Address & Billing Address

 Click on Confirm Order.You will get PO Order Number.

 By downloading our mobile app  Powertex Tools

 Enter the mobile number on the main screen.

 Click verify button. You will be prompted for password. This is the new password which will be sent to your mobile number through sms.

 Input the password sent through sms and you will be shown the menu.

 Input the scratch number

 Select the brand

 Just click on the verify button and the system will check the product information and displays you the result saying whether the product is genuine or fake.
